Volume

Stated as the space matter takes up

How do we measure it?

For a regular solid measure the dimensions with a ruler then multiply them

For an irregular solid place the object in a measuring cylinder,
and then measure the difference in the height of the water

Time

Devices such as digital stopwatches can be very precise ; Up to 0.01secs

However, the main accuracy error would be Human Error

Measuring period of a pendulum

  1. Place a mark at the pendulum's rest position
  2. make sure your line of site is perpendicular(90 degrees) to the oscillations
  3. Measure how long the pendulum takes to undergoe x oscillations (x>=10)
  4. Repeat until you find the avg time
  5. calculate the time of an individual oscillation by dividing the mean (avg) time by x
