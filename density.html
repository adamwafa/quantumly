Density

Density

Stated as, mass per unit volume of a substance

Math Relation

Mass (m) / Volume (v)

Measured in Kg/m3



Density of a liquid

  1. Find the mass of 10cm3 of the liquid
  2. Divide the mass by the volume to find the density

Density of a Regular Solid

  1. Calculate the volume of the object by finding the length of each dimension then multiplying
  2. Find the mass of the object by using a balance
  3. Divide the mass by the volume to find density

Density of an Irregular Solid

  1. Find the volume of the object by measuring the displacement in a measuring cylinder
  2. Find the mass of the object by using a balance
  3. Divide the mass by the volume to find density
