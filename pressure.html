Pressure

Pressure

is the force exerted per unit area


Pressure (N/m2 / Pa) = Force Applied (N) / Area

P = F / A



Pressure in Liquids

  • Transmitted throughout the liquid equal
  • Acts in all directions
  • Increases with depth / density
    •


Pressure (Pa) = density (kg/m3) x Gravity (N/kg) x Height (m)

P = ρ x G x H



Measuring pressure

Mercury Barometer

  • Used to measure atmospheric pressure
  • The height of mercury is directly proportional to the outside


Manometer

  • Used to measure pressure of a gas
  • Pressure of the gas is relative to the atmospheric pressure = ρ x G x H
  • Pressure of gas = ρ x G x H + atmospheric pressure
