Center of Mass

Determining the centre of mass of a "Plane Lamina"

  1. Push pin through point or edge of the lamina,
    and allow it to swing freely
  2. Use plub line to mark vertical line
  3. Repeat for a second point on edge of lamina
  4. Where two lines cross is the center of mass

The center of mass of an object is the point where mass is seemed to be concentrated,
and therefore where the weight seems to act


As such, the center of gravity affects stability, if the centre of gravity
is located outside the base then the object toppels, the lower the center of gravity
the more stable the object is
